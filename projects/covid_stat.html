About the Project

The project studies the effect of CoVID-19 on economy in Australia. The project uses the number of new cases in Australia, reflecting pandamic's situation and ASX Index, reflecting economy.

Results

Analysis

  • Economy starts decreasing significantly at the beginning of the pandemic
  • Economy tends to recover as the number of cases reduces
  • A negative correlation between ASX Index and the number of new cases per day can be observed. Pandemic has a dramatic effect on the economy
