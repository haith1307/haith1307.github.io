How long does it take to cook this?

Completed on May 2021

About

The project is Project 2 for Machine Learning COMP30027 subject at The University of Melbourne, Semester 1 2021. The goal of the project is to build a Machine Learning model that predict duration label of a given dish, given its details about ingredients and steps. The given data has the following labels: dish name, number of ingredients, number of steps, ingredients, steps and duration labels. Duration labels are nominally discretised into three levels: 0 - fast, 1 - normal, 2 - slow.

Result

The model was uploaded to Kaggle to compete with other peers' models. Result shows that our team's model rank first with an accuracy of more than 80%.
